Preview release.

Embeds FIMK 0.5 and NXT-PLUS based on NXT 1.5.13.

Fixes a bug where secret phrases are automatically trimmed.

Improved wallet/sign in handling.

responsive (mobile) support.

Experimental support for new 'free' gossip protocol.
